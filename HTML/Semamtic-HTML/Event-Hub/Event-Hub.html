Upcoming Events

The Celestial Tea Ceremony

GA mystical and ancient ritual where participants gather beneath a canopy of stars to partake in a meditative tea ceremony. Guided by a wise elder, attendees will learn the art of slow, mindful sipping while gazing at the constellations. The ceremony culminates in a collective wish whispered into the night sky, carried aloft by the gentle breeze.

The Whispering Walls Festival

A unique celebration held within an ancient labyrinth of interconnected caves. Participants are invited to leave behind their worries and troubles by whispering their concerns into the walls of the labyrinth. It is believed that the echoing whispers are absorbed by the earth, offering solace and a sense of release. The festival culminates in a communal sound bath, where the collective whispers are transformed into a soothing and harmonizing soundscape.

The Echoing Caves Festival

Embark on a journey of self-discovery and introspection at this unique festival held within a network of ancient caves. Participants are invited to explore the labyrinthine passages, leaving behind their worries and troubles by whispering them into the echoing walls. It is believed that the caves absorb negative energy, offering a sense of release and renewal. The festival culminates in a communal sound bath, where the collective whispers are transformed into a soothing and harmonizing soundscape.

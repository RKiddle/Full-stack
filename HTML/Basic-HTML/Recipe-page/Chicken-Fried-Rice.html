Chicken Fried Rice

Get ready for a flavor explosion! This chicken fried rice recipe is bursting with savory goodness and will quickly become a family favorite. It's simple to make, and you can customize it with your favorite veggies and protein.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon oyster sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onion
  • 1/2 cup chopped carrots
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 cup chopped cooked chicken
  • 2 eggs, beaten

Instructions

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and green onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the carrots and peas and cook until tender-crisp, about 5 minutes more.
  3. Add the cooked chicken and cook until heated through, about 2 minutes more.
  4. Push the vegetables and chicken to one side of the skillet and add the beaten eggs to the empty side. Scramble the eggs until cooked through, then break them up with a spatula.
  5. Add the cooked rice, soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Stir everything together until the rice is heated through and the flavors are combined.
  6. Serve immediately.
